CSR

We take ‘an actions, not words’ approach when it comes to being a socially responsible business.  This means providing legal services that not only comply with relevant laws, regulations and principles but also taking measurable and realistic steps to ensure that we continuously improve the social and environmental performance of our business.

For us, social responsibility covers five key areas – workplace, marketplace, community, charity and the environment.  The approach we take in these areas feeds into everything we do, from our company values and how we treat our employees, to being a good neighbour, our day-to-day interactions with our clients and customers and improving any environmental impact our business may have when delivering the services we provide.

We are proud of our long-standing tradition of supporting a wide variety of charitable organisations and are particularly focussed on supporting education and employability schemes within our local communities. We have in place a programme of community and charitable activity, allowing us to share skills, support community initiatives, participate in employee volunteering and contribute to assignments and collections.

We are members of the national business charity ‘Business in the Community’ and most recently became a member of the Prince’s May Day Network.

For more information regarding our charitable and community work please visit the news and publications section of our website.

Environmental Policy

We are committed to continuously improving the environmental performance of our business.  We recognise that our day-to-day activities impact on the environment in ways which are both positive and negative and we wish to minimise any harmful effects wherever and whenever practicable.

We take our responsibilities to the environment seriously.  We have implemented a firm wide Environmental Policy, endorsed by our Board.  Our Finance Director, Mark Buxton has overall responsibility for ensuring the effective implementation of our Environmental Policy.

Our Environmental Policy defines our approach to managing the environmental impact of our business and sets out objectives where we have made a commitment to improve.  We also contribute to Business in the Community’s annual Environmental Index, allowing us to track our progress and highlight areas where we can improve the future environmental performance of our business.
