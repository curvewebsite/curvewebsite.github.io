Pre Legal Collections & Recoveries

Our service is based round the use of proven letter, telephone and property visit strategies as part of a solution that involves escalating for court action on appropriate cases. We ensure our approach is tailored to the facts of each case and we operate a case ownership model when engagement with the debtor begins. Our belief is this helps achieve effective relationships and continuity for both the debtor and our clients.

Our expertise covers:

  • outsourced and overflow credit control;
  • debtor tracing/ profiling;
  • portfolio segmentation;
  • property/ site visits;
  • rehabilitation;
  • ledger collect outs.

All collections are measured against regulatory compliance frameworks as well as financial targets to ensure all collections activity is undertaken in a fully compliant and responsible manner. All practices are compliant as a minimum with FSA, OFT and SRA requirements.Â 

All our staff are trained to deal with sensitive matters such as mental health and specialist staff are deployed for commercial site visits to conduct verification audits and collection of ledgers including supporting paperwork and electronic data.

We are one of the first law firms to receive the Credit Services Association (CSA) accreditation for both our collectors and company.
