  • Secured Recoveries

    Acting for over 65 financial services brands, we have gained a thorough understanding of the business environment in which UK lenders operate and are ourselves fully compliant with industry requirements, including the FCA’s Treating Customers Fairly Principles and The Mortgage Conduct of Business Sourcebook.

    Our secured recoveries services work on either a standalone basis or as part of a wider range of integrated services, giving our lender clients the advantage of being able to tailor a recovery solution which suits them, whilst also providing the best mix of expertise, a single point of contact and true economies of scale.

    Our secured recoveries services include:

    • Early arrears management
    • Trace and profiling
    • Title Defects
    • Repossession litigation and sales
    • Assisted voluntary sales
    • LPA receivership
    • Mortgage shortfall
    • Professional negligence

     Best Law Firm of the Year 2012 and 2013

    Awarded ‘Best Law Firm’ by Mortgage Finance Gazette for two years running based on the strength of our relationship with our lender clients.


  • Unsecured Recoveries

    Our unsecured recoveries expertise covers trace and profiling through to complex or sensitive litigation actions with a UK or international element.   Our recoveries services include:

    • Trace, profiling and investigation
    • Collections
    • Litigation

    All our services are compliant as a minimum with FCA, OFT and SRA requirements and can be integrated at any stage to varying degrees with our clients’ own internal processes, or we can provide a complete outsourcing solution.

    We work closely with our clients to devise the most effective recoveries strategies for different debt types.  Our expertise extends from collections activity, where the primary objective is to prioritise the debt and obtain settlement, through to litigation, including a straightforward money judgment service, to dealing with contentious debt or debt subject to counterclaims, where prior experience and a detailed understanding of particular legal issues are essential.


  • Legal Property Services

    We have provided market leading legal property services to lenders, intermediaries, estate agents and house builders for over 25 years, handling conveyancing, remortgage and commercial property transactions across the UK.

    We work hard to deliver a range of legal property services which meet the commercial needs and expectations of our clients, whilst also making sure that the customer experience is nothing less than outstanding.

    As a consistent ‘top panel performer’, we are well practised in handling volume fluctutations without the need to outsource to third parties, assuring a consistent high quality service and customer experience at all times.  We pride ourselves on being easy to do business with and make use of a number of current technologies to facilitate this, ranging from online and mobile platforms to bespoke and flexible case management systems.

    Our legal property services include:

    • Residential and commercial sale and purchase
    • First time buyers
    • Remortgage
    • New build
    • Buy to let
    • Repossession sales
    • Shared ownership
    • Assents

    Our aim is to make the legal property transaction as smooth as possible for all parties concerned with the shortest possible timescale.
