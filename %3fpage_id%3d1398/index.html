Asset Finance Litigation

Our dedicated asset finance teams in Bradford and Glasgow have been providing litigation services to motor and other asset finance clients for over 10 years.  We act for leading brands, including premium motor finance brands and are consistently the top performer in our clients’ supplier performance league tables.  The types of issues handled by our teams include:

  • Return of Goods claims;
  • shortfall recoveries;
  • ad hoc advice on regulatory issues including relevant FSA and OFT guidance;
  • challenges to standard terms and conditions;
  • innocent purchaser disputes;
  • guarantee claims;
  • consumer challenges, including challenges to the validity of an Agreement under the Consumer Credit Act, PPI claims and ‘misselling’/misrepresentation claims;
  • Fraud claims.

As well as litigating on behalf of our asset finance clients, we can also provide advice on and draft documentation which is subject to the provisions of the Consumer Credit Act 1974 and the OFT Debt Collection Guidance.  We also undertake process reviews for clients, to ensure that their internal processes are as efficient as possible and fully compliant with the FSA’s regulatory regime.

For further information, please contact Senior Litigation Partner, Philip Robinson.
