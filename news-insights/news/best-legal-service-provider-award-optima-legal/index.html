Best Legal Service Provider Award for Optima Legal

Tags: 
Property

Optima Legal has won the ‘Best Legal Services Provider’ Award at the 2011 ‘What Mortgage’ Awards.

What Mortgage is the UK’s number one monthly mortgage magazine whose philosophy and aim is to help consumers make the best choices by providing them with the most comprehensive information when making their home-buying decisions.

The What Mortgage awards have been running annually since 1993. The awards are designed to recognise outstanding contributions by product and service providers with the home-moving and mortgage sector and includes categories such as ‘best national bank’, ‘best national building society’ and ‘best overall lender’.

Mark Tosetti, Director of Marketing says ‘We’re delighted to win such a prestigious award. This further confirms Optima Legal as a leading legal service provider to both lenders and consumers. We pride ourselves on our high levels of service and this award confirms the great work our employees carry out each and every day’.

Commenting on the award Ben Wilkie, Editor of What Mortgage adds ‘Our panel of judges voted Optima Legal the winner because of its commitment to the customer. It offers good value products without scrimping on the quality of its service. Its friendly, helpful team provides support and information in the best possible way at what can be a stressful period in their customers’ lives.”
