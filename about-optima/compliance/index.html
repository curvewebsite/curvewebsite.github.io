Compliance

As a law firm we are regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and are bound by the SRA Code of Conduct. In addition the majority of our clients are regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority and therefore expect us as legal service provider to give assurance that we are giving a fully compliant service, for example in accordance with the principle of Treating Customers Fairly or under the FCA’s Mortgage Conduct of Business. We do this by operating a central compliance team which is dedicated to implementing and monitoring firm-wide adherence to our regulatory compliance framework. The team is headed by Kate Wright, Partner.

The team is also responsible for maintaining our certification under ISO 9001, business continuity management, professional indemnity insurance and the development of firm-wide training on regulatory issues such as Data Protection and Money Laundering.
