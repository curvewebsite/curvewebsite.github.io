The Firm

We are a UK wide commercial law firm providing property and recoveries services for UK businesses from our offices in Bradford, Newcastle and Glasgow. 

With over thirty years experience, we pride ourselves on providing a technically excellent service whilst always remaining open to new ways of working with clients.  We listen to them, build our services around their individual requirements and use their feedback to continuously improve our services and performance.  Our aim is to become a trusted advisor to our clients and we encourage them to pick up the phone to us, to discuss ideas, whether this is on a technical point of law or to get our opinion on a proposed new strategy. 

To ensure that our clients need not hesitate to speak to us, we avoid the ‘hourly rate’ pricing model of most law firms wherever possible, as we recognise that clock watching does not encourage ongoing dialogue.  We believe that charging clients on hourly rates should be the exception rather than the rule.  We offer flexibility on both reporting and fee arrangements and are valued by clients for being approachable and generous with the ‘added value’ time we invest to ensure we deliver results.    

Our clients include major UK banks, insurance companies, large retailers and manufacturers as well as public sector institutions and SMEs.  We have particular expertise in the financial services sector and were voted ‘Law Firm of the Year’ at the Mortgage Finance Gazette awards 2012.
