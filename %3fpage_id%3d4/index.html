Credit Management & Recoveries Services

We provide a full range of credit management and recoveries services to lenders and other businesses operating in the financial services sector as well as businesses operating within insurance, manufacturing, construction, utilities, distribution, recruitment, healthcare, and education. Our client base in the financial services sector alone extends to over 65 financial services brands, including most high street lenders.

Our expertise covers trace and profiling through to complex or highly sensitive litigated actions with a UK or international element. 

We operate in England, Wales and Scotland through our offices in Bradford, Newcastle and Glasgow, whilst meeting international recovery needs via an overseas network. 

Underpinning all services is our dedication to:

  • delivering best results cost effectively through a partnership approach to working with clients;
  • being both approachable and generous with the ‘added value’ time we invest to ensure we deliver best results.

We are ranked tier one for debt recovery services by the Legal 500 (Yorkshire and Humberside) and maintain a number of representative body memberships/ associate memberships including the Debt Buyers and Sellers Group, Finance and Leasing Association and the Council of Mortgage Lenders.

For further information, please contact Senior Litigation Partner, Philip Robinson.
