We look to build long-term relationships with all our clients. Our development process allows us to respond and pro-actively support evolving business strategies.

Open Source Community Involvement

Back in 2008, our MD Peter McClory focussed on Drupal as a scalable, secure platform for building versatile content management systems. Later he went on to found the Drupal Yorkshire user group. Over the past three years, hundreds of Drupal developers and community members at our monthly events, from beginners to global experts. If you're interested in finding out more about the Drupal community, or want to extend your knowledge of Drupal, head along to one. They are free to attend and scheduled every month on 2nd Wednesdays.

We are joint organisers for Northern User Experience (NUX) UX community based in Leeds, Manchester and Newcastle, helping to organise the next UX camp, and bimonthly meetups with the Leeds UX community.

We also sit on the steering committee for Yorkshire Digital. A hub for the growing digital community in Yorkshire. The Curve team regularly attends and speaks at local events such as ITProfs, Agile Leeds, Forefront Leeds, HeyStac, and many other active and innovative technology groups.
