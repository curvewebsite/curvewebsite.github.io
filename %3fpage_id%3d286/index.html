Legal notices

Optima Legal is a trading name of Optima Legal Services Ltd, a limited company registered in England and Wales (Registered No. 05781608, VAT No. 882657186). Our registered office is at Hepworth House, Claypit Lane, Leeds
LS2 8AE. We are authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority in accordance with the SRA Code of Conduct 2011, which can be viewed at www.sra.org.uk.

A list of Shareholders and Directors is open for inspection at its registered office.  We use the word `Partner` to refer to a Shareholder or Director of the company, or an employee or consultant with equivalent standing and qualifications.  Only persons designated as `Solicitor`, `Associate`, or `Partner` are qualified Solicitors.

Our partners and employees are authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority but a number of our lawyers are also members of other bodies including the Council for Licenced Conveyancers and the Institute of Legal Executives.

Insurance

Optima Legal Services Ltd maintains professional indemnity insurance arranged by Lockton Companies International, The St Botolph Building, 138 Houndsditch, London, EC3A 7AG. Details of the insurers and the territorial coverage are available for inspection at our registered office.

Quality of service

We are committed to providing a high quality legal service to our clients and customers. As an overriding principle we believe in “Treating Customers Fairly”. We monitor customer satisfaction in various ways and use feedback to review our processes and improve our service. We operate a quality management system which is certified under ISO 9001:2008. We may record telephone calls for training and quality purposes.

Any client or customer who is not satisfied with any aspect of our service or our invoice should try to resolve this with the partner responsible for the work.

If the client or customer is not fully satisfied with the outcome of the complaint they may refer the matter to the Legal Ombudsman, PO Box 6806, Wolverhampton, WV1 9WJ.

WARNING – Scam Communications

We are aware that the Optima Legal name has been used in a fraudulent email scam involving a purported lottery prize fund. Please note that Optima Legal Services Limited has no involvement with these emails and any other scams.

These scams aim to obtain money or your information for illegal purposes. If you are uncertain about any communication purporting to be from Optima Legal, Optima Legal Services Limited or any of its partners or employees, and wish to check it if is genuine, do not reply to it. Please forward the communication to info@optimalegal.co.uk and we will advise.

Please see the Solicitors Regulation Authority website for further guidance on scams.
