Drupal 8 site launched for University of York

1st December, 2015

We just launched a Drupal microsite for the University of York research group New Economic Models and Opportunities for digital Games (NEMOG).

We developed a Drupal 8 site for University of York's New Economic Models and Opportunities for digital Games (NEMOG). The research group needed a new website with an events page, publications available for download, and a members area. It also represents a new visual identity which Curve have designed for NEMOG, including branding guidelines and their new logo.

The project was completed in less than four weeks, and involved extensive collaboration with a large team of stakeholders. This ensured that project requirements were met, the new brand design, and all content was ready for a solid stint of quality assurance testing prior to the launch.
